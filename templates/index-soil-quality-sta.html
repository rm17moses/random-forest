About Soil Quality Standards

The Minister of Water and Environmental Affairs has drafted national norms and standards for the remediation of contaminated land and soil quality in the Republic of South Africa, under section 7 (2) (d) read with section 73 (1) (a) of the National Environmental Management: Waste Act (Act No. 59 of 2008). These standards are designed to provide a uniform national approach to the remediation of contaminated land and soil quality. They aim to limit uncertainties about assessment criteria and methods, as well as establish minimum standards for necessary environmental protection measures during remediation activities. The norms and standards apply to any person who undertakes any remediation activity within South Africa.
